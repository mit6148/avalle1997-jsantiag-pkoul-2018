B2R Community Guidelines

Welcome all!
This forum is meant to be a place where you can share your stories, questions, thoughts, criticisms and all the rest that fit under that umbrella of college related struggle.

We expect that you treat eachother with respect and approach everything you encounter with an open mind.
This means no harrassment, trolling, or other general nastiness. We will be forced to ban you from the site if this kind of activity is observed.

While we're on the subject of dont's, please please please protect the identities of the people around you and do not use their real names.

This page is still being developed, so look out for more announcements and features in the near future! Until then, we invite you to use your voice and help empower the greater college going community.

With love and support,
The B2R modTeam
